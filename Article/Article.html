Rainier named favorite beer amongst Gonzaga Students

Flurnsday, Smarch 33rd 2019

Rainier Beer is named after Mount Rainier

By Christian Kruep

After a recent poll issued to all Gonzaga Students of legal age and known owners of fake IDs Rainier Beer came out on to top as the most liked cheap beer of all.

Beating out such pereniall favorites Keystone Light and Rolling Rock, the beer brewed with Yakima Valley Hops was polled as being the prefered cheap beer of atleast 66% students who completed the poll.

"I'M THE RAINIER MAN! I LOVE RAINIER" said "The Rainier Man" while wearing a t-shirt emblazoned with Rainier's signature red R along with a red flannel. "ITS THE BEST!" he elaborated while passing out Rainier cans to random strangers like a Spokane Johnny Appleseed. He was unavailable for further comments after passing out in a bush on Mission Avenue. Its said you can summon the Rainier Man by finding the Rainier and saying "Brewed with Yakima Valley Hops" three times fast while looking at it.

Rainier is alleged to have some medicinal values. One Dr. R is a propent of this stance. "I love Rainier beer because of its high vitamin R content. Each can contains 100% of your daily Vitamin R needs." Dr. R said at his office in the Logan. When directly asked what Vitamin R exactly is, Dr. R declined to answer and then jumped out a window while yelling "CHEESE IT."
